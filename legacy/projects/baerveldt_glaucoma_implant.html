Baerveldt Implant
Implantation of a Baerveldt® BG 101-350 Glaucoma tube shunt to treat congenital glaucoma, as performed by Dr. Ike K. Ahmed.
Surgical · Photoshop

FIGURE 1. Eyelids are retracted by barraquer wire speculum. A 6-0 Vicryl traction suture is placed in the periphery of the cornea in the superior region. The eye is moved down to expose the superior temporal quadrant. An incision starts at the midline of the quadrant, superior to the cornea, and dissects away the conjunctiva and Tenon's capsule laterally along the contour of iris, sparing the sclera.
