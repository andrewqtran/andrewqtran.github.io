jQuery Gallery

The Projects gallery was built using jQuery. All content is loaded and hidden until shown by clicking a thumbnail, which hides all the other thumbnails and moves itself to the top left corner of the section. Clicking "Return to Gallery", or "Projects" from navigation bar (usability testing repeatedly showed users tend to click here to go back), resets the section to its original state.
