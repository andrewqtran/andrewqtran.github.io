3D Still Life
A 3D still life composition of various biomedically themed objects.
Biomedical · Maya & Mudbox

The vertebrae, aortic clamp, flask, and heart were modeled and textured from scratch. The liver was 3D scanned and the hip bones were extracted from a CT scan via OsiriX; both were cleaned up, retopologized and textured in 3D Coat, Maya and Mudbox.
