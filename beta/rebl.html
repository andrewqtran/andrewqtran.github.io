We R DNA n Art

That's my full name, spelled backwards. Aptly, my passions lie in healthcare, science, tech, and design. Coming from a psychology background and preclinical oncology research, I took a big leap into medical illustration the moment I discovered it. In grad school, I learned more about community-centered design, in addition to various visualization approaches.

The experience only deepened my love for both healthcare/science and design/art.

I strive to create impactful and meaningful solutions that put the human element at the core. I've been fortunate to be able to connect the dots in my past for this purpose. Have a look at how these experiences have made me a better designer.

When I'm not facing a screen, I read (OK, that's still a screen), try DIY projects, stare into the ocean, go swimming, running, hiking, or travel.
